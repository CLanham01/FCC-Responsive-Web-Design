King of Kings: Jesus Christ

Worthy of Worship!

  • Much is unclear of the timing of His birth. All that is curtain is what was written in the gospels.

  • History can attest to the impact of Jesus’ life. The world was forever changed.

  • Although many have and yet still do misrepresent His name and do evil acts in His name, His true character is described in the Gospels and the rest of the New Testament of the Bible.

  • His character is LOVE!

  • "For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life." – John 3:16 NIV

  • While being with God in the beginning (John 1:1-5) The Word (Jesus) became flesh and while being 100% God and 100% Man Jesus lived a perfectly righteous life as an example to all what living with the Holy Spirit is like.

  • Though being innocent of any wrongdoing, He was taken and suffered and was crucified in place of all mankind’s sins.

  • After 3 days He rose from the grave conquering Death, Hell, and the Grave.

  • He ascended into Heaven to intercede on behalf of His followers with the Promise to return one day.

He is truly worthy of worship!

There is no greater love than to lay down one’s life for one’s friends.

-- John 15:13 NLT

More information can be found in the New Testament in the Bible. It is recommended to begin in the Gospel of John.
